## 3. CORE OFFERINGS

### Section Headline
**Two Disciplines. One Mission. Infinite Possibilities.**

---

### MUAY THAI — The Art of Eight Limbs

**Fight with precision. Move with purpose. Own your power.**

Muay Thai isn't about anger or aggression. It's about control, focus, and mastery. 

You'll learn punches, elbows, knees, and kicks — not to hurt anyone, but to discover what your body can do when it moves with intention.

Train with a world-class fighter who believes technique beats brute force every time. Build fight IQ, not ego. Walk in as a beginner. Walk out as someone who can't be shaken.

**What You'll Experience:**
- Traditional Muay Thai techniques with modern coaching
- Pad work, shadow boxing, clinch training
- Cardio, conditioning, and controlled power
- Mental discipline and unshakable confidence

**Who It's For:**  
Anyone ready to feel strong, focused, and fearless — no experience required.

---

### AERIAL DANCE — The Art of Flight

**Defy gravity. Express yourself. Find your flow.**

Aerial dance is where strength meets art, where you stop asking "can I?" and start asking "how high?"

From silks to lyra, pole to hammock, you'll build muscles you didn't know you had and move in ways that feel like flying.

This isn't circus tricks. It's movement as self-expression. It's play, creativity, and the kind of strength that sneaks up on you while you're having fun.

**Disciplines We Teach:**
- Lyra (aerial hoop)
- Silks
- Hammock
- Pole
- Aerial cube
- Straps

**What You'll Experience:**
- Foundations in technique, conditioning, and flow
- Creative choreography and self-expression
- Full-body strength and flexibility training
- A community that celebrates every victory

**Who It's For:**  
Anyone who's ever wanted to feel weightless, powerful, and free.

**Certified Instructor. Competitor. Believer in Impossible Things.**

Tinsley has spent 5+ years in Bangkok's aerial dance scene, competing in three major competitions and mastering nearly every apparatus you can hang from.

She teaches seven aerial disciplines — lyra, silks, hammock, pole, aerial pole, cube, and straps — with the kind of patience and encouragement that makes you forget you're upside down for the first time.

**Movement Style:** Graceful. Bold. Fearless.

## VOICE & TONE GUIDELINES

### Do's:
✅ Use active voice  
✅ Short, punchy sentences  
✅ Aspirational but grounded  
✅ Inclusive language ("you," "we," "your body")  
✅ Celebrate effort, not just achievement  
✅ Use power words: unlock, discover, master, become, transform  

### Don'ts:
❌ Corporate jargon  
❌ Fitness clichés ("no pain no gain," "beast mode")  
❌ Gendered language  
❌ Intimidating or exclusive tone  
❌ Overpromising unrealistic results  
❌ Body-shaming or appearance-focused messaging

## COLOR-CODED USAGE GUIDE

**Black** — Primary backgrounds, headlines, grounding elements  
**Neon Green** — Muay Thai branding, CTAs, energy moments  
**Electric Pink** — Aerial dance branding, highlights, joy moments  
**White** — Body copy, breathing room, clarity
